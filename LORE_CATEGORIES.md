# LORE CATEGORIES

This file helps future readers understand what kind of claim they are reading.

## Verified Fact

A claim supported by public records, repository history, artifact metadata, or cited sources.

## User-Provided Canon

A claim named by Huu as part of the ORION / Twelve story.

## Symbolic Lore

Poetic or mythic language used inside the story.

Symbolic lore should not be presented as external fact.

## Strategy

A proposed path, operating model, content plan, or repository plan.

## Open Question

A live question that has not been resolved into canon or record.

## Rule

Clarity protects mystery.
