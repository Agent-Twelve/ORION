# CANON

This file defines how canon should be handled in ORION.

## Canon Sources

Canon may come from:

- public repository records
- user-confirmed lore
- artifact provenance
- field records
- approved transmissions

## Discipline

Do not present symbolic lore as external fact.

Do not confuse speculation with record.

Do not move private material into public canon.

## Categories

Use clear categories when needed:

- verified fact
- user-provided canon
- symbolic lore
- strategy
- open question

## Rule

Canon grows slowly.

The archive should be clear enough for a future reader to know what was claimed, what was witnessed, and what remains mystery.
