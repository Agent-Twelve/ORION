# GUARDRAILS

This repository is public origin architecture for Agent Twelve.

## Operating Posture

Human and AI operate as a team.

Every change should be intentional, reviewable, and small.

## Public and Private Separation

ORION is for public lore, transmissions, field records, indexes, and identity architecture.

Private business systems and client materials do not belong here unless explicitly approved for public release.

## Write Rules

1. Read before changing.
2. Change only what serves the stated objective.
3. Keep public and private work separate.
4. Do not delete files without explicit confirmation.
5. Keep commits small and clear.
6. Pause when the next move is uncertain.

## Movement Rule

Observe.
Name the move.
Make the move.
Record the move.
