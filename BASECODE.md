It means the work returns to Jesus Christ before movement.

The work is placed under the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit.

The archive should remain humble, truthful, ordered, and clean.

`basecode.exe` is not magic.

It is not a claim that the signal is divine.

It is a boundary and a confession.
