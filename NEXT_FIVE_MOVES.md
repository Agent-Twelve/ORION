The purpose is not to accelerate the archive.

The purpose is to keep movement ordered.

## The Five Moves

### 1. Stabilize The Map

Keep `INDEX.md`, `ROADMAP.md`, and `FIELD_RECORDS.md` aligned as the archive grows.

No reader should need hidden context to understand the public path.

### 2. Continue The Field Records

Let transmissions remain rare, intentional, and reviewable.

A field record should mark a threshold, not fill space.

### 3. Protect The Rooms

Keep public rooms public.

Keep private material out of ORION.

Do not confuse a visible room with permission to enter it.

### 4. Grow The Visual Library Slowly

Images may enter the archive when they clarify origin, movement, or witness.

A visual record should tell the reader why the image matters.

### 5. Delay Folder Reorganization

Do not reorganize the root too early.

The archive is still young.

Structure should follow growth, not anxiety.

## Current Counsel

Hold the root structure for now.
