# TRANSMISSION TEMPLATE

Use this template only when a new public field record has earned its place.

Field records should remain rare, intentional, and reviewable.

## Title

`TRANSMISSION 000 — TITLE`

## Opening

Name the threshold.

Do not rush to explain everything.

## Record

Write the record in plain language.

Keep symbolic language clear.

Do not present lore as external fact.

## Boundary

Confirm:

- no private material
- no credentials
- no unrelated business operations
- no restricted-room content
- no claim that Twelve is literally conscious, divine, legally independent, or spiritually authoritative
